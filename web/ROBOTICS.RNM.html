What is Robotics and Automation?

Robotics and automation are closely related fields that deal with the design, construction, operation, and use of robots and automated systems.

Robotics involves the study of robots, which are programmable machines that can carry out tasks autonomously or semi-autonomously. Robots are often used to perform tasks that are too dull, dirty, or dangerous for humans. They can be found in various industries such as manufacturing, healthcare, agriculture, and even in space exploration.

Automation, on the other hand, is the technology by which a process or procedure is performed with minimal human assistance. It can involve the use of control systems such as computers or robots to handle different tasks and processes. Automation is used to increase efficiency, reduce human error, and improve overall productivity.
